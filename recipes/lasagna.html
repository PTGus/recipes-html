Lasagna

About this recipe

This beloved lasagna recipe is a hearty, slow-simmered classic that layers rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and perfectly melted mozzarella into a comforting, oven-baked masterpiece. Built on a flavorful base of Italian sausage, ground beef, tomatoes, herbs, and garlic, the sauce cooks low and slow to develop deep, savory richness. Combined with tender noodles and three cheeses, it creates a dish that’s indulgent, aromatic, and incredibly satisfying.

Though it takes time and care to prepare, the result is well worth the effort: a beautifully structured lasagna with balanced seasoning, a luscious texture, and a taste that only gets better the next day. Whether served at a family gathering or enjoyed as leftovers, this recipe lives up to its reputation as one of the most celebrated and reliable lasagnas ever shared online.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
